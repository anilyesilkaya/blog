Anil Yesilkaya is a senior software engineer at MathWorks, where he works with the WLAN, 5G, and 6G teams, focusing on AI for wireless communications. Prior to joining MathWorks, he was a postdoctoral researcher on Light Fidelity (Li-Fi) under Prof. Harald Haas at the University of Strathclyde. He holds a PhD in digital communications from the University of Edinburgh, Scotland.
